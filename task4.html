TYTO THE SWIFT

Reflexes and finesse

Tyto the Swift is a danger at close-range and deals heavy damage with deadly melees. As you'll see in the following spotlight video, Tyto is true to his name with blazing speed, swift movements and killer precision as he more than likely appears as a blur to his opponents.

Play Style

Tyto rely on their high mobility to be able to dash into combat, deal some blows and then get out of melee as soon as possible. However, as soon as someone catches them, they struggle to mitigate damage well enough to survive. But in the hands of a discerning player, Tyto can be one of the best competitive heroes in Gigantic.

  • Stay elusive by combining a dodge with every attack.
  • Upgrade fang to close down foes or bypass enemy armor.
  • Your blade dance is a whirlwind that can deflect incoming projectiles.
